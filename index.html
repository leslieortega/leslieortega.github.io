About Me

About Me

Intro: Hi! My name is Leslie Ortega and welcome to my digital portfolio and website. I grew up as a city kid in northern New Jersey in a Latino household. I moved to Colorado to pursue an education in an environmental and geospatial field which is when my love for the outdoors flourished. I currently reside in Bryce Canyon National Park, Utah with my cat Neka.

Professional Interests: My passions lie in geospatial design, drone/UAV technology, and AI and machine learning applications. I am particularly interested in aerial camera systems, mobile mapping platforms, and photogrammetry software. I have always been intrigued by the development of innovative geospatial technologies that utilize real location intelligence to help businesses make informed decisions.

Education: I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Geography and Geographical Information Systems, from the University of Colorado Boulder (2019). I have over 7 years of professional experience which I gained during and after my Bachelors. I am a geospatial analyst, GIS consultant, land surveyor and aspiring aerial photogrammetrist.

Personal Hobbies: In my spare time, I enjoy any physical activity in the great outdoors, be it hiking, biking, running, climbing or gardening. On calmer days, I enjoy reading while hammocking, bird watching, thrift shopping, and cooking different cuisines. Recently, I've been learning how to renovate RVs.

Industry Knowledge

Industry Knowledge

Data Collection

I gained extensive fieldwork experience during my time as a senior survey technician for a small land surveying and civil engineering firm. Through this position I learned the best practices for data collection, problem solving in the field, and how to collect data with the purpose of an end deliverable.

Database Management

As a GIS Consultant at Bryce Canyon National Park I assist with the development and implementation of improved spatial data management. In addition, I maintain the legacy resources dataset for source accuracy and necessary metadata in order to improve data collection workflows. I am proficient in geospatial data management, including working with geodatabases and performing database queries. I’m familiar with many different GIS spatial data file formats including shapefiles, imagine, geotiff, and file geodatabases.

Spatial Analysis

I have dived into spatial analysis by manipulating and analyzing geographic data in Python which creates a more efficient and reproducible environment for doing GIS. My experience includes managing large sets of spatial data with geopandas, managing coordinate reference systems, overlay analysis and network analysis. I have experience working on solution-oriented apps using a wide range of technologies such as Python, PostGIS, SQL, APIs and more.
