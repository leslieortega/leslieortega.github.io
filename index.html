About Me

About Me

Intro Hi! My name is Leslie Ortega and welcome to my digital portfolio and website. I grew up as a city kid in northern New Jersey. I moved to Colorado to pursue an education in an environmental and geospatial field which is when my love for the outdoors flourished. I currently reside in Bryce Canyon National Park, Utah with my cat Neka.

Professional Interests My passions lie in geospatial design, drone/UAV technology, and AI and machine learning applications. I am particularly interested in aerial camera systems, mobile mapping platforms, and photogrammetry software. I am intrigued by the development of innovative geospatial technologies that utilize real location intelligence to help businesses make informed decisions.

Education I am a geospatial analyst, GIS consultant, and land surveyor. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Geography and Geographical Information Systems, from the University of Colorado Boulder (2019). I have over 7 years of professional experience which I gained during and after my Bachelors.

Personal Hobbies In my spare time, I enjoy any physical activity in the great outdoors, be it hiking, biking, running, climbing or gardening. On calmer days, I enjoy reading while hammocking, thrift shopping, and cooking different cuisines. Recently, I've been learning how to renovate RVs.
